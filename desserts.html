INFORMATION ON DESSERTS

Pound Cake

Indulge in the delightful richness of homemade pound cake, available in both vanilla and chocolate flavors. This beloved classic dessert boasts a dense yet moist texture, making every bite a heavenly experience. Our vanilla pound cake delights with its subtle yet distinct vanilla flavor, while the chocolate pound cake satisfies chocolate lovers with its decadent cocoa goodness.

These scrumptious cakes not only offer a heavenly taste but also provide a source of energy and essential nutrients. Pound cakes are typically made with butter, eggs, sugar, and flour, providing a good amount of carbohydrates and protein. However, they should be enjoyed in moderation, as they are also high in calories and saturated fat. Whether enjoyed plain or dressed up with a dollop of whipped cream or fresh fruit, our pound cakes are sure to satisfy your sweet cravings and leave you wanting more.

Peanut Brittle

Indulge your taste buds with the delightful crunch of homemade peanut brittle, while also benefiting from its nutritional value. This classic confection combines the irresistible combination of sweet caramelized sugar and roasted peanuts, making it a source of energy and essential nutrients. Peanuts are packed with healthy fats, protein, and dietary fiber, which contribute to a feeling of fullness and aid in digestion. However, it's important to enjoy peanut brittle in moderation, as it is also high in sugar and calories. So, savor this timeless delight as an occasional treat, appreciating both its delectable taste and the nutritional benefits it provides.
