INFORMATION ON DISHES

Plantain Chips

Plantain Chips is a popular snack in African countries like Ghana. here at Small Bites Africa, OUr plantain chips come in 2 flavors: Sweet and Savory.

Our Sweet plantain chips are made from ripe plantain. The ripe fruit allows for the sweet taste and can be combined with some other spices such as to bring out more of the flovor that it holds.

Our Savory plantain chips are made from unripe plantain. Although not eaten raw, unripe plantains are perfect for crispy and crunchy chips when fried and salted to taste.

Chin Chin

This is a classic West African snack, similar to the Sacndinavian snack, Klenat. It's a crunchy, fried dough that is usualy made with a smooth nutmeg flavor. The dough is usually kneaded into small cubes about an inch wide and a quarter-inch thick before frying.
