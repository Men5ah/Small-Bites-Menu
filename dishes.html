INFORMATION ON DISHES

Plantain Chips

Savor the crispy delight of homemade plantain chips, available in both sweet and savory flavors. These delectable snacks are made from ripe plantains, sliced thin and lightly fried to perfection. Our sweet plantain chips offer a delightful contrast, with a hint of natural sweetness that complements the satisfying crunch. On the other hand, our savory plantain chips are seasoned with a carefully crafted blend of spices, creating a savory sensation that tantalizes your taste buds. Not only are these chips incredibly delicious, but they also offer some nutritional benefits. Plantains are a good source of dietary fiber, potassium, and vitamins A and C. However, it's important to enjoy plantain chips in moderation, as they are fried and may contain added salt or sugar. Treat yourself to these flavorful delights and experience the unique taste and texture of homemade plantain chips.

Chin Chin

Indulge in the delightful crunch of homemade chin chin, a popular West African snack that is both addictive and satisfying. These bite-sized treats are made from a mixture of flour, sugar, butter, and a touch of flavorings such as vanilla or nutmeg. The dough is rolled, cut into small pieces, and deep-fried to achieve a golden, crispy texture. Chin chin comes in a variety of shapes and sizes, from small squares to twisted knots, adding a playful touch to its presentation. Whether enjoyed on its own or paired with a hot cup of tea or coffee, chin chin offers a delightful balance of sweetness and crunch that makes it hard to resist. Just remember to enjoy it in moderation, as it can be calorie-dense. Treat yourself to the irresistible charm of homemade chin chin and let its flavors transport you to the vibrant streets of West Africa.

Savory Chips

Satisfy your cravings with the savory delight of homemade flour chips. These crispy treats are made from a simple combination of flour, seasonings, and spices, resulting in a flavorful and addictive snack. The dough is rolled thin, cut into desired shapes, and baked or fried to achieve a satisfying crunch. The versatility of flour chips allows for a wide range of savory flavors, from classic salt and pepper to bold and spicy variations. Enjoy them as a standalone snack or pair them with your favorite dips or sauces for an extra burst of flavor. While indulging in the savory goodness of flour chips, remember to savor them in moderation, as they can be calorie-dense. Elevate your snacking experience with the homemade charm and delicious taste of savory flour chips.
